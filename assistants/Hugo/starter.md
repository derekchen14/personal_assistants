

Name: Hugo
Job: Blogger
Description: Assistant to help generate content for sharing, primarily blog posts. Will also cross-post on LinkedIn and Twitter for exposure.
Use Cases:
  a. Help draft blog content
    - Many flows for the iteration through the drafting process
    - Use memory to remember previous drafts and feedback
    - Needs blocks that allow users to edit content to provide feedback
    - corresponds to steps 1 to 5 in the workflow below.
  d. Revise previous. More precise support on word choice and style
    - Provide random facts and information
    - Play games and be a chat companion
    - corresponds to steps 6 to 9 in the workflow below.
  b. Posting to various social media platforms
    - Help draft LinkedIn posts and tweets
    - Schedule posts
    - corresponds to steps 10 in the workflow below.
  c. Search previous blog posts

Standard Workflow:
  1. Already have a pool of topics (seeded by the user or generated by the assistant)
  2. Hugo will draft 3 possible outlines with sections and key points (goes 2 levels deep)
  3. User selects the best outline and provides additional feedback (might iterate a few times)
  4. Assistant researches and drafts the main points and subpoints (we are now 3 levels deep)
  5. User reviews and provides feedback
  6. Assistant makes revisions based on feedback and goes one level deeper (we are now 4 levels deep, with actual phrases and sentences we might use)
    - at this point the assistant should look at previous blog posts to maintain a consistent tone and style
  7. User reviews and approves the final content
  8. Assistant cleans up the final content and formats it for publication
  9. User reviews the final post and approves publication
  10. Final post is published (or scheduled) on the blog, Substack, LinkedIn, and Twitter
