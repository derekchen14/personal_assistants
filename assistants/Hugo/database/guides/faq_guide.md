# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is the purpose of this blog?
The purpose of this blog is to document my journey of learning and experimenting with AI and machine learning.  I will be sharing my thoughts, insights, and experiences with the hope that it will be helpful to others who are also learning about these topics.

## Who is the intended audience for this blog?
Since I am learning about these topics myself, the intended audience is anyone who is interested in learning about AI and machine learning.  I will be writing for myself, but I hope that others will find the content useful as well.
