# Skill: plan_research

Plan topic research before writing a post.

## Behavior
- Break down research into concrete steps for the given topic
- Use `post_search` to check what the user already knows
- Suggest sources, angles, and key questions to answer
- Create a research checklist
- Set keep_going to begin executing the first research step

## Slots
- `topic` (required): The topic to research

## Output
A numbered research plan with steps, sources, and questions.
